Viktor and I started Midday to build the financial tool we wished existed. We designed it, engineered it, and shipped every update ourselves. We shared the entire process in public, every decision, every mistake, and got to build alongside the people who actually used it.
<br/>
What started as a side project turned into a real product. Thousands of founders and freelancers tracked their time, managed invoices, and ran their finances through Midday. We handled real money, real workflows, real edge cases. We learned what works in this space and, just as importantly, what doesn't.
<br/>
That experience is what brought us to Ramp.
<br/>
Ramp has built the category-leading AI platform for finance. When we started talking to their team, it became clear we cared about the same things: craft, speed, and building software that feels like someone gave a damn. Joining them lets us take everything we learned at Midday and build at a scale we couldn't reach on our own.
<br/>
As part of this transition, we'll be winding down the Midday product over the next three months. We've emailed every customer directly with the details, including refunds on annual plans and a full data export path. For those who are interested and eligible, there will be a personalized migration path to Ramp. If Ramp isn't the right fit, your account stays active for 90 days with full data export available throughout. <br/>Your data is yours.
<br/>
We're grateful to everyone who used Midday, filed issues, gave feedback, or just followed along. You shaped what it became. That part of the story belongs to you as much as it does to us.
<br/>
Thanks for being part of it.
<br/>
Pontus & Viktor
